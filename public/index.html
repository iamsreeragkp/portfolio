About Me

I'm Sreerag K P, a software engineer who enjoys building things end to end — from native Android apps to backend APIs. My work centers on Kotlin and the Android/Jetpack ecosystem, extends into React Native for cross-platform mobile, and rounds out with TypeScript and Node.js/Express on the backend.
